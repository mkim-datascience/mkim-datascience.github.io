About Me

My STEM professional friends sometimes share their concerns about research-related writing and presentations with me. Despite their outstanding technical abilities, they find that their writing and presentations don't always yield the expected results.

I've accumulated extensive writing and presentation experience in both academic and industrial settings, engaging with people from diverse backgrounds. I've helped resolve my friends' concerns by sharing the expertise I've gained through these experiences.

Fortunately, I've always received positive reviews, which has given me the confidence to cautiously begin offering this service, hoping that it might be helpful to others experiencing similar difficulties.

Currently, this is still in a preliminary testing phase, and I'm providing it at no cost for now, as I also value the opportunity to learn about different research areas through our interactions. I would deeply appreciate any honest feedback about the service that you might be willing to share.
